About EL

Ell is a cloud kitchen with easy takeaway or delivery ordering system and fast service. Our goal is to deliver hygienic, delicious , healthy food and refreshments wherever you need it : be it at your home, workplace or in gym.

We mix emotions and fresh ingredients in our food , energy and protein in our refreshments and care in our packaging and service. Our drinks and dishes will recharge you for the rest of the day and allow you to live it to the fullest. We know that you are worthy of the best service, so the best is what we provide.
